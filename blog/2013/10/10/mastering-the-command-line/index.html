Mastering the Command Line

Oct 10th, 2013

One of the basic and most important tools for new coders to learn is the command line. It’s how you talk to your computer to: change directories, creating/delete files, locate a file, just to name a few.

In class we are learning about the command line on this site:

http://cli.learncodethehardway.org/book/

It’s easy to follow along and it has you run commands that will be the most useful.

Another helpful site:

http://cli.learncodethehardway.org/book/

I’m a big fan of cheat sheets!
